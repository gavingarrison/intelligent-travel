# Mission

Help people confidently spend their limited leisure time and money on experiences worth remembering.

## Product Meaning

The product is not content volume, search traffic, affiliate conversion, or itinerary generation. The product is confidence.

Every page, recommendation, map, guide, and AI response should reduce uncertainty for a person making a meaningful leisure decision.

## Operating Standard

Every recommendation should answer:

> Is this worth my time?

That answer must include reasoning, caveats, confidence, and verification status.
