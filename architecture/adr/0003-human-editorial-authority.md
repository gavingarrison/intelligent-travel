# ADR 0003: Preserve Human Editorial Authority Over Recommendations

## Status

Accepted

## Context

AI can organize, retrieve, synthesize, and personalize information, but the brand's trust depends on human taste, experience, and judgment.

The company should not become a generic AI itinerary generator.

## Decision

AI systems may assist recommendation delivery, but final recommendation eligibility and public editorial verdicts are human-governed.

The AI layer must expose:

- Citations
- Editorial confidence
- AI confidence
- Verification status
- Caveats

## Consequences

Benefits:

- Protects trust
- Keeps brand differentiation
- Reduces hallucinated recommendations
- Supports transparent explanations

Tradeoffs:

- Slower content expansion than fully automated systems.
- Requires editorial operations and reverification workflows.
- AI product scope must respect access policy and confidence boundaries.
