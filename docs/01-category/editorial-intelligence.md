# Editorial Intelligence

Editorial Intelligence is the category we are building.

It combines:

- Human judgment
- Structured knowledge
- Transparent methodology
- Repeatable evaluation
- AI-assisted retrieval and explanation

## Difference From Reviews

Reviews produce averages. Editorial intelligence produces judgment.

## Difference From Search

Search exposes options. Editorial intelligence narrows options with rationale.

## Difference From AI Planning

Generic AI creates plausible answers. Editorial intelligence grounds answers in reviewed canonical knowledge.
