# Brand Operating Brief

## What The Company Is

This company is an editorial intelligence company for leisure, travel, hospitality, and meaningful time.

It begins as an editorial publication organized by regions and locations, with focused verticals such as family travel, boba, wine, coffee, restaurants, hotels, parks, museums, and seasonal experiences.

It grows into a trusted intelligence layer for how people spend leisure time well.

The company should be built for a 20+ year horizon.

## What We Sell

We do not sell travel, boba, wine, restaurants, hotels, or experiences as commodities.

We sell:

- Confidence
- Judgment
- Taste
- Discernment
- Time well spent

The business earns trust by helping people make better decisions about experiences that matter.

## Audience Need

People have more information than ever and less certainty than they want.

They can find endless options, reviews, itineraries, influencers, and AI-generated suggestions. What they still lack is trusted judgment about what is actually worth their time, money, attention, and family energy.

## Brand Position

The brand should feel:

- Experienced
- Tasteful
- Calm
- Discerning
- Useful
- Independent
- Long-term
- Editorially serious

It should not feel:

- SEO-first
- Affiliate-driven
- Generic
- Trend-chasing
- Listicle-heavy
- Volume-optimized
- Algorithmically shallow

## Editorial Standard

Recommendations should be deeply experienced, opinionated, transparent, contextual, structured, and explainable.

The goal is not to recommend everything. The goal is to recommend only what deserves recommendation.

## Durable Question

Every review, guide, rating, itinerary, and recommendation should answer:

> Is this actually worth your limited time?

## AI Standard

AI should amplify editorial judgment. It should never replace editorial judgment.

Humans remain authoritative for taste, nuance, emotional value, aesthetics, surprise, cultural understanding, and final recommendations.

AI can assist with organization, research, personalization, synthesis, retrieval, pattern recognition, and recommendation delivery.
