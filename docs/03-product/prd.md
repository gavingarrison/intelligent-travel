# Product Requirements

## Product North Star

- Increase trust.
- Reduce decision fatigue.
- Create memorable experiences.

## Interfaces

- Website
- Ghost
- AI Advisor
- Maps
- Mobile Apps
- Newsletter
- Internal Editorial Workbench
- APIs

All interfaces consume one canonical knowledge graph.

## Initial Vertical Slices

### Big Sur Hotels

Ship guides, maps, structured hotel cards, editorial verdicts, and AI support.

### San Gabriel Valley Boba

Ship location-first boba discovery, shop cards, taste notes, comparison modules, and map support.

### Paso Robles Wine

Ship wineries, wine producers, tasting context, itinerary support, maps, and editorial caveats.
