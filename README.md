# Intelligent Travel

Active workspace: `/Users/gavingarrison/Documents/Intelligent Travel`

This repository is the technical and editorial foundation for a long-horizon company in leisure, travel, hospitality, and trusted experience intelligence.

The company begins as an editorial publication organized by regions and locations, with focused verticals such as family travel, boba, wine, coffee, restaurants, hotels, parks, museums, and seasonal experiences. Over time, it should evolve into a structured intelligence platform that helps people confidently spend limited leisure time and money on experiences worth remembering.

We are not building another travel website. We are building a trusted judgment system for meaningful leisure.

## Core Thesis

The company does not sell travel. It sells confidence, judgment, taste, and better decisions about time.

Modern leisure discovery creates too many options and too little certainty:

- Search creates options.
- Reviews create averages.
- Algorithms create popularity.
- AI creates plausible answers.

The opportunity is trusted editorial judgment: structured, explainable, experienced, and useful.

## Operating Standard

Every recommendation should answer one question:

> Is this actually worth your limited time?

That standard applies to hotels, restaurants, resorts, wineries, coffee, museums, road trips, destinations, architecture, parks, family experiences, seasonal events, and leisure rituals.

## Product Direction

The company should evolve through six phases:

1. Editorial publication
2. Structured editorial database
3. Editorial knowledge graph
4. Contextual recommendation engine
5. Personal leisure advisor
6. Leisure Intelligence Platform

Locations are the organizing spine. Verticals are the editorial lenses.
