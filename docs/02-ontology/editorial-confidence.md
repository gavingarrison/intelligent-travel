# Editorial Confidence

Editorial Confidence measures how strongly the company stands behind a claim.

## Inputs

- Firsthand experience
- Recency
- Repeat visits
- Reviewer expertise
- Corroborating evidence
- Volatility of the place or experience
- Known caveats

## Statuses

- candidate
- desk_researched
- firsthand_current
- firsthand_stale
- needs_reverification
- recommended
- recommended_with_caveats
- not_recommended
- retired

## Rule

AI confidence cannot upgrade editorial confidence.
