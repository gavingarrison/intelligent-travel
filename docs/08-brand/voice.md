# Voice

## Voice Principles

- Calm
- Clear
- Opinionated
- Specific
- Honest about uncertainty
- Useful before clever

## Avoid

- Hype
- Generic luxury language
- SEO filler
- Affiliate pressure
- Fake certainty
- Overly chatty AI phrasing

## Example

Prefer:

> Beautiful, expensive, and not the easiest fit with toddlers.

Avoid:

> A must-visit luxury escape perfect for everyone.
