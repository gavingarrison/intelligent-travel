# Doctrine

## Core Beliefs

- Confidence is the product.
- Judgment compounds.
- Taste can be modeled.
- AI should amplify editors.
- Every recommendation deserves a rationale.
- Relationships are more valuable than records.
- Time is more valuable than money.

## What We Are Not

- Travel blog
- Review site
- OTA
- Affiliate site
- Generic AI planner
- Scraped-content SEO operation

## Canonical Rule

Ghost publishes editorial content. It does not own the company's knowledge.

The canonical knowledge graph is the company memory.
