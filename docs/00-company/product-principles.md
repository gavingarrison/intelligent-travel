# Product Principles

- Start with trust, not monetization.
- Make uncertainty visible.
- Preserve reasoning with every recommendation.
- Organize around regions and locations, then verticals.
- Treat taste as structured but not mechanical.
- Use AI for retrieval, synthesis, and drafting, not final authority.
- Make relationships first-class.
- Build reusable knowledge, not one-off pages.
- Favor practical editorial workflows over abstract modeling purity.
- Do not overbuild Phase 1.
