# Vision

Become the world's trusted operating system for leisure decisions through editorial judgment, structured knowledge, and AI.

## Long Horizon

The platform should support decades of accumulated judgment across regions, locations, verticals, people, taste profiles, and experiences.

## What Success Looks Like

- Readers trust the brand more than generic reviews.
- Editors compound judgment into structured knowledge.
- AI can retrieve, explain, and personalize without inventing.
- Locations become intelligible through vertical lenses.
- Relationships between places become more valuable than isolated records.
